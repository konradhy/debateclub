### **Concession & Pivot** (Defensive)
**Formula:** "You're right that [X], but here's what you're missing: [Y]"

**Examples:**
- "You're absolutely right that transition costs money. But here's what you're missing: we're already paying $165 billion annually in climate disasters."
- "I agree that economic disruption is a concern. But the real question is: disruption now, or collapse later?"

**When to use:** When AI says "costs too much" or "economic disruption"

---

**Deploy specific data when making points**

**Key Receipts:**
- **IPCC 2023:** "Delaying action increases costs 2-3% per year"
- **NOAA 2022:** "$165 billion in climate disasters"
- **NASA:** "Arctic ice melting 13% per decade"
